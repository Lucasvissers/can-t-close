# info
### how to install?
1. get your friend's pc
1. Download the zip from [GitHub](https://github.com/Lucasvissers/can-t-close)
1. Unzip
1. put the file called "speed-up pc.vbs" on his desktop.
1. Wait for him to open it.

### How to close?
1. Open taskmanager(ctrl+shift+esc)
1. choose the task en end it
